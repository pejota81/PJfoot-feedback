# MISTER v0.23 - Logout & Date of Birth Fixes

> Released: 2026-05-16

Quality of life improvements and bug fixes for account management.

---

## Features

- **Sign out button:** Added dedicated logout button to Main Menu for quicker session termination without opening Account Settings.

## Bug Fixes

- **Date of Birth persistence:** Fixed redundant date normalization that was causing unexpected values in the account profile DOB field.

## Technical

- Removed double normalization in `buildUserProfile` to ensure stored dates are returned as-is after initial validation.
