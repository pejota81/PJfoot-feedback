# PJfoot v0.08 — Transfer Market Overhaul & Free Agency

> Released: 2026-05-02

---

## Transfer Windows

Contracted players can now only move during official transfer windows. Free agents remain signable at any time.

- **Summer window** — weeks 1–5 (season start)
- **Winter window** — weeks 20–23 (mid-season)
- A coloured banner in the Transfer Market screen shows whether a window is open or closed
- Contracted players are hidden from the market when no window is active

---

## AI Transfer Listings

When a transfer window opens, AI clubs automatically list surplus and ageing players for sale.

### Criteria for Listing

An AI club will mark a player as available if any of the following apply:

- Age ≥ 32
- Contract expires at end of season
- Player is in the bottom 20% of the club's squad by skill
- Random 10% churn (any player may be listed)

Up to 4 players per club are listed per window. All listings are cleared when the window closes.

---

## AI Offers Inbox

AI clubs can now make bids for your listed players.

- Offers arrive each week while a transfer window is open
- Each offer displays the bidding club, fee, and expiry week
- **Accept** — player transfers immediately, fee added to your balance
- **Reject** — offer is removed; the club may bid again
- Offers auto-expire after 2 weeks if unanswered
- Access your inbox via the **Incoming Offers** tab in the Transfer Market screen

---

## Loan System

You can now loan players out to AI clubs for half a season (19 weeks).

- Loan fee is paid upfront (10× weekly salary)
- Loaned players show a **LOAN** badge in the Squad screen and cannot be toggled for sale while on loan
- Players automatically return to your squad when the loan expires

---

## Advanced Filters

The Transfer Market browse screen now includes:

- **Min skill** slider
- **Max age** filter
- **Max salary/wk** filter
- **Free agents only** toggle
- Name and position filters (unchanged)

---

## Free Agency Overhaul

### Starting Free-Agent Pool

On a new game, **80 synthetic free agents** (age 23–34, skill 60–85) are injected into the market so there are interesting options immediately available.

### AI Signing Rules

- AI clubs in the **top 5 divisions** (global IFFHS rank ≤ 5) do **not** sign free agents — they use the paid transfer market only
- Lower-division AI clubs start signing free agents after **week 5** of each season (giving the player a 5-week head start)
- A second signing pass runs at **week 20** (start of winter window period)
- AI aggression ramps up over 4 seasons: season 1 is very passive (signs only if squad < 12), reaching full aggression by season 4 (signs if squad < 22)

### Player Free-Agent Limit

- The player-managed club can sign a maximum of **5 free agents per season**
- The remaining slots are displayed in the Transfer Market banner as **FA Slots: X/5**
- The Sign button is disabled (with a tooltip) when the limit is reached
- The counter resets at the start of each new season

---

## Contract Expiry & Retirement Fixes

- Players whose contracts expire at season end are now immediately released as **free agents** (previously they remained at their club)
- Mandatory retirements (age > 38) are now replaced **1-for-1** with 18-year-old youth players — no cap
- Youth players are also added in the **first season** to replace mandatory retirements
- Free agents who reach retirement age are removed from the player pool during season aging

---

## Breaking Change — Saved Games

Existing saves load correctly. Two new fields are auto-migrated:

- `season.pendingOffers` — defaults to `[]`
- `season.freeAgentsSigned` — defaults to `0`

No data loss; old saves continue working without a new game.

---

## Season Budget Reset

Transfer and wage budgets are now recalculated at the start of every season based on the club's actual financial situation.

### Transfer Budget

The board now allocates the **higher** of:

- **15% of expected season income** (prize money + matchday) — the board-floor allocation
- **30% of current club balance** — rewards clubs that have accumulated wealth

A club that has saved up from a good run of results will have a meaningfully larger transfer war chest next season.

### Wage Budget

Unchanged — calculated as **70% of expected season income ÷ total weeks**. This reflects the club's ability to sustain ongoing wages, not its cash reserves.

Both counters (`transferSpent`) reset to zero at the start of each new season.
