# MISTER v0.27 - Match Engine Momentum + Confidence + Home Pressure

> Released: 2026-05-17

Minor release focused on core gameplay simulation updates for match feel and tactical context.

---

## New

- **Momentum-aware player performance:** per-player match-day performance now incorporates club momentum, with higher momentum nudging average performance up and reducing volatility.
- **Attendance-driven opportunity split:** home and away teams no longer receive equal attack counts every match; home opportunity share now increases with attendance.
- **One-match confidence effects:** attackers who scored in the previous match and goalkeepers coming off a clean sheet now receive a short-lived positive performance boost in their next match.

## Technical

- Updated `src/engine/matchSim.ts`:
  - Added momentum and confidence inputs to the per-player performance factor.
  - Switched to fixed-total, attendance-weighted attack allocation (home share scales from 50% to 66%).
  - Updated event minute derivation to support variable attack counts per side while keeping timeline bounds coherent.
- Updated `src/engine/seasonManager.ts` to derive and persist last-match confidence flags from completed weekly league and cup matches.
- Updated `src/types/player.ts` with confidence fields:
  - `lastMatchScored`
  - `lastMatchCleanSheet`
