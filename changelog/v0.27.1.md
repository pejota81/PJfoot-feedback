# MISTER v0.27.1 - Calendar Sequencing & Transfer UX Clarity

> Released: 2026-05-17

Patch release focused on season-calendar sequencing rules and transfer-screen clarity.

---

## Fixes

- **League rematch ordering:** teams now play all opponents once before any second-leg rematch.
- **Home/away sequencing:** fixture generation now prioritizes home-away alternation and constrains venue streaks to at most two consecutive home/away games when a valid schedule exists.
- **Transfer market clarity:** Transfers screen now shows a clear squad-full warning banner with a direct link to Squad when Team A reaches the roster cap.

## Technical

- Reworked `src/engine/fixtures.ts` scheduler:
  - Enforced strict first-leg then second-leg generation.
  - Replaced cross-leg round swapping with seeded round-order search and streak-aware home/away orientation.
- Updated `src/components/screens/Transfers.tsx`:
  - Added a visible squad-cap state banner and quick navigation action.
